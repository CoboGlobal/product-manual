### <u>**Deposit from external wallets**</u>

1. Select the token type for the transfer. Selection is limited to tokens included in your organizationâ€™s subscription package. To enable a new token type (fees apply), click **Not enabled**.
2. Click **View Deposit Addresses** to display the deposit address for the selected token.
3. (Optional) If you require a new deposit address, click on **Add Address** to create one.
4. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
5. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you wish to deposit into.

To learn how to deposit into your MPC Wallet, see [Transfer](https://cobo-6.mintlify.app/cobo-portal/transfer/introduction).
