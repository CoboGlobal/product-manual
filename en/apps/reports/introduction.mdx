Reports is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/apps/introduction) developed by Cobo.  It provides transaction reports within a specified timeframe and asset holdings reports at a specific time across Custodial Wallets and MPC Wallets.

## Key features

### Professional use cases
Export reports for purposes such as tax reporting, reconciliation, and submitting financial documentation to regulatory entities or other institutions.

### Standardized format
Reports are exported in a standardized, user-friendly PDF format, ensuring complete audit trails and data accuracy.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/reports/form.png" className="full_screen" alt="PDF report example"/>

### Secure and convenient sharing
Share via PDF download or online link (recipients can both view and download reports through the shared link). Password protection, if enabled when generating reports, applies to all access methods.
