Staking is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/apps/introduction) developed by Cobo. To install Staking, follow the instructions in [Browse and install apps](/en/apps/browse-and-install).

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/staking/set-up.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Image showing Staking as a Cobo Portal App."/>

<Info>The Staking app supports staking BTC (Babylon) and ETH (Beacon Chain) within [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) in [Organization-Controlled Vaults](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/create-mpc-vault).</Info>
