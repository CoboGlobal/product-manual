Staking is a [Cobo Portal App](/en/portal/portal-apps/introduction) developed by Cobo. You can use it to manage asset staking with protocols like the Babylon protocol in your Cobo Portal wallets.

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/staking/staking-in-app-store.png" className="screenshot_modal" alt="Image showing Staking Appears as a Cobo Portal App."/>

## What is staking?

Staking is a process where you earn rewards by locking up your tokens to support a blockchain network's security and operations. Cobo's Staking App supports different networks and assets.

## Key features

### Comprehensive asset overview and monitoring

Gain full control over your staking operations with an integrated view of all staked assets, detailed information for each asset type, and real-time monitoring of status and rewards. Stay informed with full transparency on your staking positions and activity history.

### Streamlined staking process

Easily stake and manage your assets with a few clicks on an intuitive interface that clearly displays staking status and assets available to stake, unstake, or withdraw. 

### Validator selection and customization
Optimize your staking strategy by selecting or customizing validators to enhance your potential rewards.

### Robust security
Safeguard each staking operation using Cobo Portal's user role checking mechanism and risk control policies, with dedicated support for any inquiries.

### Staking API
Integrate staking into your systems with the Cobo WaaS 2.0 API for enhanced flexibility and control over staking activities.
