Screening automatically scans every transaction for your selected [MPC Wallets](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/introduction) and [Web3 Wallets](/en/portal/custodial-wallets/web3-wallets/set-up). You can modify which wallets are monitored at any time:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login)
2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/> to open Cobo Portal Apps
3. Find **Screening**
4. Click the gear icon beside the app as shown in the following image:

<img src="/en/images/portal-apps/screening/screening-apps-page.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Image showing Screening Appears as a Cobo Portal App."/>

5. In the **App Settings** section, click **Edit**
6. In the **Wallet Scopes** section:
   - Select MPC Wallets and Web3 Wallets to enable transaction scanning
   - Deselect wallets to disable transaction scanning
7. Click **Confirm**, then click **Confirm** again to apply your changes

<Note>Changes to monitored wallets may require approval based on your organization's [governance policies](/en/portal/organization/governance-intro).</Note>
