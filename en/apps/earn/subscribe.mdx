<Note>Prerequisite 1: You have created a Custodial Wallet and deposited assets to be invested. You have added the wallet in [**Wallet Scopes**](/en/apps/app-settings) accessible by Earn.</Note>

<Note>Prerequisite 2: You need to have the **Spender** or **Admin** role to invest in products.</Note>

1. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_apps.svg" className="icon" alt="the Apps icon"/> on [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login), find **Earn**, then click **Open** to open Earn.
2. Click **Subscribe** under **Actions** in the row for your chosen product.
3. In the **Subscribe** window:
   - Select a wallet type and a wallet.
   - Enter the investment amount.
   
   After you have entered the amount, the following information will be displayed:
      - **Estimated Rewards**: Annual estimated rewards based on the APY and investment amount
      - **Subscription Date**: The next day at 00:00 after your subscription
      - **Rewards Start Date**: The next day at 00:00 after your subscription
      - **First Distribution Date**: The next day at 00:00 after your subscription

4. Review and check the box to agree to the terms and conditions.
5. Click **Submit**. Confirm the transaction in your Cobo Guard app.

After subscription, you can track your investment status on the [investment details](/en/apps/earn/view) page.
