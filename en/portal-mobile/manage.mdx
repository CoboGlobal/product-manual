<Warning>To use the Cobo Portal Mobile App, your device must have a screen lock enabled. Make sure the screen lock stays enabled — disabling it will prevent you from using the app.</Warning>

After completing the first-time setup, you can use the Cobo Portal Mobile App (hereinafter referred to as Portal Mobile) to manage your account, assets, and approvals anytime, anywhere. This document introduces the main screens and management features of the app.

## Bottom navigation

The main screen of Portal Mobile has four bottom tabs:

- **Wallets**: View the wallet list and assets, and initiate receiving and transfers.
- **Approvals**: View and handle requests awaiting your approval.
- **Transactions**: View transaction history.
- **Me**: Manage your device, key shares, and settings.

<Note>If you selected **View only** mode when logging in, "View only mode" is shown at the top of the screen. In this mode, you can only browse assets and history, and cannot sign or approve. To gain full permissions, tap **Bind this device**.</Note>

## Wallets

In the **Wallets** tab, you can:

- View the wallet list, token holdings, and balances in your organization;
- Open wallet details to view the breakdown of individual tokens;
- **Receive**: Display wallet addresses and receiving QR codes;
- **Transfer / withdraw**: Initiate a transfer, review the transaction details, and sign with biometrics.

## Approvals

In the **Approvals** tab, you can efficiently manage approval requests:

- **Pending**: Displays all requests awaiting your approval;
- **All**: Displays all approval requests regardless of their status.

Tap a request to view its details. For transaction requests, you can review the transaction content, source, risk control information, and so on, then choose to **Approve** or **Reject**. For MPC transactions, after the approval passes you receive a signing request in Portal Mobile and complete the signing.

Key share generation requests are also shown in this tab. For how to handle them, refer to [Generate and back up your key shares](/en/portal-mobile/generate-and-back-up-key-shares).

## Transactions

In the **Transactions** tab, you can view transaction history and the details of individual transactions, including transaction type, chain, amount, transaction fee, initiator, and recipient.

## Me

In the **Me** tab, you can manage your device and settings.

### This device

- **My Public Key**: View and copy your public key. The public key represents your identity and is used in scenarios such as multi-factor authentication (MFA) and operation approval.
- **Key shares**: View your key share information, including the TSS Node ID, key groups (Main Group, Signing Group, Recovery Group), and the details of each group. If a backup is outdated, you can update the backup here.
- **Bind device**: If the current device is not yet bound, you can complete binding here.

<Note>Key share information is only visible if you hold key shares.</Note>

### Settings

- **Appearance**: Switch the app appearance between system, light, and dark.
- **Language**: Switch the app language between system, Chinese (Simplified), and English.
- **Export logs**: Export local logs for a selected date to help with troubleshooting. Local logs are retained only for the most recent few days.
- **Test messages**: Send and receive test approval messages to verify that the approval channel works properly.

### Support and about

- **Contact support**: Join the Cobo community on Discord, or contact support by email.
- **About**: View the app version number and privacy policy.

## AI assistant

Portal Mobile includes a built-in AI assistant. Tap the **Ask AI** entry to open a chat window, where you can ask questions in natural language, get supplementary information related to your account, transactions, and approvals, and get assistance handling approval requests within the conversation.

For details on the AI assistant's capabilities, interaction, and capability boundaries, refer to [Cobo Portal Mobile App AI assistant](/en/portal-mobile/assistant).

## Other common features

- **Scan QR code**: Quickly link Cobo Portal (web) with this device by scanning a QR code, enabling passwordless login and other operations.
- **Notifications**: View push notifications from Cobo Portal, including approval requests, key share generation requests, and more.
- **App lock**: Portal Mobile automatically locks after being in the background for a period of time, and requires biometric authentication to re-enter.
