## What is the AI assistant?

The AI assistant is a conversational AI tool built into the Cobo Portal Mobile App (hereinafter referred to as Portal Mobile), powered by Cobo AI. You can ask questions in natural language and get supplementary information related to your account, transactions, and approvals. In approval scenarios, the AI assistant also helps you understand the business background and potential risks of an approval request.

The AI assistant does not replace human judgment, nor does it change accountability for approval decisions.

## What it can help you with

In Portal Mobile, the AI assistant can help you with the following:

- **Answer questions:** Ask questions in natural language to learn about your account, wallets, transactions, and approvals.
- **Understand transaction content:** Rephrase structured transaction information into natural language and explain key fields, helping you quickly understand the intent of a transaction.
- **Flag potential risks:** Check whether a destination address or contract has known risk events or abnormal markings, and provide corresponding alerts.
- **Explain triggered rules:** Describe the risk control rules triggered by the current transaction and their logic, helping you understand why an approval request was generated.
- **Assist with approvals:** Surface pending approval requests in the conversation, where you can approve, reject, or modify them directly.

## How to use it

1. Tap the **Ask AI** entry in Portal Mobile to open the AI assistant chat window.
2. Ask the AI assistant questions via text or **voice input**.
3. To provide more context, take a photo with the **camera** or add a **file** attachment.
4. If multiple agents are available, you can switch to the agent you need within the conversation.
5. Past conversations are saved under **History**, where you can view, rename, or delete them at any time.

During the interaction, the AI assistant combines your current account state and data to provide explanations and tips directly relevant to your question.

### Using the AI assistant in approvals

When a conversation involves an approval request, the AI assistant displays related information and analysis for that request. You can:

- View the AI assistant's interpretation of the approval request and its risk alerts;
- Approve, reject, or modify the approval request directly in the conversation;
- For operations that require additional confirmation, complete biometric verification as prompted.

The AI assistant does not automatically advance the approval process — all approval and signing actions still require your manual confirmation.

## Capability boundaries

The AI assistant provides decision-support information, not automated approval or execution capabilities.

- It does not replace human judgment, approval, and signing; it does not automatically approve, reject, or submit approval requests, nor trigger subsequent operations;
- It does not bypass existing permission controls, risk control policies, or approval processes;
- Its output is based on currently available data and configuration, and may be incomplete or contain judgment errors — you should make decisions based on the actual situation;
- Its output is for reference only and does not constitute financial, legal, tax, or compliance advice.

If an AI assistant response has issues, you can submit feedback via the **Feedback** entry in the conversation to help us continuously improve.
