<Warning>
**Before you download — Cobo Portal Mobile is currently for NEW USERS ONLY.**

Cobo Portal Mobile **only supports newly registered Cobo Portal users / new organizations** at this stage. If your account is **already bound to Cobo Guard**, downloading and installing Portal Mobile will **not** let you bind your device or migrate from Cobo Guard. **No migration path from Cobo Guard to Portal Mobile is available yet.** Existing Cobo Guard users should continue using Cobo Guard and wait for the official migration path. See [Migrate from Cobo Guard](/en/portal-mobile/migrate-from-guard) for details.
</Warning>

<Note>Cobo Portal Mobile supports both **iOS and Android** devices. For specific operating system version requirements, refer to Portal Mobile's page in the App Store and Google Play.</Note>

Cobo Portal Mobile is published in the App Store and Google Play under the name **Cobo Portal**. Choose the download method that matches your device type.
