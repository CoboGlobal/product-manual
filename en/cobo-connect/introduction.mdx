<Note>We recommend using [WalletConnect](/en/walletconnect/introduction) whenever possible for better multi-chain support and overall user experience. Cobo Connect will be gradually phased out based on actual usage and will remain available during the transition period.</Note>

Cobo Connect is a Chrome extension that enables you to connect your [Cobo Portal](/en/portal/introduction) wallets with dApps. After you make the connection, Cobo Connect will identify each transaction and message from the dApps and process them based on your pre-configured [risk control policies](/en/portal/risk-controls/introduction) in Cobo Portal.

<img src="/en/images/cobo-connect/extension.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="Cobo Connect extension" />

## Key features

### Seamless integration

Easily connect your Cobo Portal wallets with dApps and enable smooth interaction.

### Enhanced security

Ensure that transactions and messages from dApps to your Cobo Portal wallet addresses are processed according to your pre-configured risk control policies in Cobo Portal.

### User-friendly experience

Manage your Web3 interactions conveniently with an intuitive interface.
