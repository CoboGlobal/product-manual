Click on the corresponding icons to manage your organization members:

- **Invite Members** <img src="/en/images/organization/invite.svg" className="icon"></img>: Add new members to your organization by sending an invitation to their email address.
- **Resend Email** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_resend email.svg" className="icon"></img>: Resend the email invitation to the selected member.
- **Modify User Role** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Edit the role of the member.
- **Delete** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_delete.svg" className="icon"></img>: Remove the member from your organization.
- **Freeze** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_freeze.svg" className="icon"></img>: Temporarily restrict the member's access to the organization account's features.
- **Unfreeze** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_unfreeze.svg" className="icon"></img>: Restore the member's access to the organization account's features.
- **Cancel** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_cancel.svg" className="icon"></img>: Cancel the invitation.
- **Submit for Approval <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_submit for approval.svg" className="icon"></img>**: Submit the request to add a new member to the organization's admin for their approval.
- **Filter** <img src="/en/images/organization/icon_filter.svg" className="icon"></img>: Tailor your view by filtering members based on name, status, role, or last activity time.
