**Applicable to: All wallets**

All tokens and chains supported by your Cobo Custodial Wallet subscription plan are displayed in the wallet interface. You can also check the current list of supported tokens and chains in [Supported tokens and chains](/en/portal/supported-tokens-and-chains). If your subscription plan does not include the token/chain you need, or if it is not yet supported by Cobo, please contact Cobo Support at [help@cobo.com](mailto:help@cobo.com).
