**Applicable to: Custodial Wallets (Asset Wallets)**

Auto token sweeping for Custodial Wallets (Asset Wallets) is handled internally by Cobo. It automatically consolidates funds from deposit addresses into the designated address/wallet. You do not need to enable a switch or manage the sweeping process.
