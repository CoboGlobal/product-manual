This article lists error codes you may encounter when using the Developer Console and Cobo Portal APIs, along with their causes and how to resolve them.

## Error 12007

Error 12007 has two possible causes:

- **Asset not found**: The requested token or asset could not be located for this operation. Verify the token, chain, and wallet in your request.
- **Gas sponsorship depleted**: When the feature that pays gas fees with your USD stablecoin balance is enabled, this error is also returned if the balance available for gas payment has run out.

To resolve the gas sponsorship case:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click your name in the bottom left corner, then select **Organization** > **Fee Station**.
3. Top up your USD or USD stablecoin balance, then retry the operation.

## Error 30038

Error 30038 indicates that your Fee Station balance is insufficient to cover the requested fee, so the operation cannot proceed.

To resolve this:

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click your name in the bottom left corner, then select **Organization** > **Fee Station**.
3. Complete a deposit to top up your balance. For deposit options, refer to [How to use Fee Station](/en/portal/fee-station/set-up).
4. Retry the operation after the deposit is credited.
