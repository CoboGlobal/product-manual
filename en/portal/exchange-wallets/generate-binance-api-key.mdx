Generate the **API Key** and **API Secret Key** for your Binance account by following these steps:

1) Log in to your Binance account.
2) Navigate to **Account** > **API management**.
3) Click **Create API**.
4) Select **System generated**, and click **Next**.

<img src="/en/images/exchange-wallet/binance2.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

5) Tick the following **4 API restrictions**:
    - Enable Reading
    - Enable Internal Transfer
    - Permit Universal Transfer
    - Enable Withdrawal
6) Select **Restrict access to trusted IPs only**.
