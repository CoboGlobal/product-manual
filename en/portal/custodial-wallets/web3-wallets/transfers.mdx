## Deposit from external wallets

1. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).
2. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **Custodial Wallets**. Select the **Web3 Wallets** tab, then select a wallet.
3. Select the **Addresses** or **Tokens** tab, navigate to the address or token for your deposit, and then click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_deposit.svg" className="icon"></img>.
4. Copy the deposit address. Please ensure that the deposit address is accurate to prevent any transfer errors.
5. Proceed to your preferred external wallet to initiate the transfer to the deposit address you have copied.

## Deposit from another wallet created on Cobo Portal

1. To open the **Transfer** dialog, you can choose one of the following ways:
    - Option 1: Complete step 1 to step 3 in the previous section, and click **Deposit From Other Wallets**. In the dialog, the token or address will be preselected for you.
    - Option 2: Click <img src="/en/images/transfers/transfer-button.svg" className="icon" alt="The transfer button" /> in the main menu on the left-hand side of Cobo Portal. Fill in the fields manually.
2. For more information about each field, see [Introduction to Transfer](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/portal/transfers/introduction).
