#### Simplified Payment Process
Fee Station automatically handles your Cobo bills, preventing overdue payments and eliminating the need to track outstanding bills.

#### Automatic Payment of Transaction Fees for MPC Wallets

Fee Station can be configured to automatically cover transaction fees for your MPC Wallets. This feature eliminates the need for ongoing balance monitoring and manual top-ups, ensuring that your MPC transactions are always covered.
