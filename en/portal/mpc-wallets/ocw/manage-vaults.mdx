1. Follow the steps to navigate to your vault:

    a. Log in to [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).

    b. Click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/wallets-icon.svg" className="icon"></img> > **MPC Wallets**, and you will see your default vault. 

    c. To switch to another vault, click <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/switch.svg" className="icon"></img> next to your default vault. In the pop-up dialog, select the vault you want to manage.

        <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/switch vault.png" className="screenshot_modal"></img>

2. To set this vault as your default for future logins to MPC Wallets, enable **Set as Default**.
    <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/set default.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"></img>

3. On the vault page, the following management options are available.
    <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/vault-page.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"></img>

    <Info> Only the vault admin has the permission to modify the MPC vault name, and export addresses and balances.</Info>

    - **Export Addresses & Balances** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/export-address.svg" className="icon"></img>: Export details such as the list of addresses under your vault and their corresponding balances. The button will be visible only after the successful creation of the Main Key.
    - **View Transactions** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon"></img>: View all transactions associated with your vault. The button will be visible only after the successful creation of the Main Key.
    - **Key Share Management** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/manage-key-share-group.svg" className="icon"></img>: View root extended public keys associated with each vault. Manage the Main Key, Signing Keys, and Recovery Keys. For more information, see [Manage keys](/en/portal/mpc-wallets/ocw/manage-key-share-groups).
    - **Edit Vault Name** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon"></img>: Modify the name of your vault. Each vault name must be unique within the organization and does not exceed a character limit of 30.
    - **Search** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_search.svg" className="icon"></img>: Locate a wallet in the vault by wallet name.
    - **Sort** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_sort.svg" className="icon"></img>: Sort the wallets in the vault by created time.
    - **Grid View** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_grid.svg" className="icon"></img>: Displays the wallets in the vault in a grid, with each entry containing information such as the number of addresses and the value and number of tokens in the wallet.
    - **List View** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_list.svg" className="icon"></img>: Displays the wallets in the vault in a list, with each entry containing information such as the number of addresses, the value and number of tokens, last activity time, and available actions.

        In List View, click the icons in each wallet's row to perform the following actions: 

            - **View Transaction History** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_view transactions.svg" className="icon" alt="View transaction history"></img>: View transaction history of the wallet.
            - **Edit Wallet Name** <img src="/en/images/mpc-wallet/icon_edit.svg" className="icon" alt="Edit wallet name"></img>: Edit the wallet name.
            - **View Details** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_view.svg" className="icon" alt="View details"></img>: View details of the wallet.
            - **Cobo Connect** <img src="/en/images/common/icon_cobo_connect.svg" className="icon" alt="Cobo Connect"></img>: Launch Cobo Connect.
