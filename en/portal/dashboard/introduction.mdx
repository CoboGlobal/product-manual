Cobo Portal's Dashboard is your central hub for monitoring and managing your digital assets. It provides a comprehensive overview of your portfolio and operations, including the following sections:

- **Quick Snapshot**: View your Total Assets, Total Deposits, and Total Withdrawals at a glance.
- **Total Assets Trend**: Track your total asset value over time with Hourly, Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Year views. Use the date picker to select a specific date range and the currency selector to view values in USD, BTC, or ETH.
- **Plan Usage**: Monitor your plan usage per wallet type, including Custodial Wallets, MPC Wallets, and Exchange Wallets.
- **Portfolio**: Explore your asset distribution across Assets, Wallets, Chains, and Exchanges tabs.
- **Cobo News**: Stay up to date with the latest Cobo ecosystem updates.

<Note>The asset value is captured daily at 23:59:59 UTC+8.</Note>
