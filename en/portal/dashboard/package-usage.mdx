Track the usage of your pricing plan throughout each billing period. Usage is shown per wallet type, including Custodial Wallets, MPC Wallets, and Exchange Wallets. Billing metrics include outgoing transfer volume, assets under custody, wallet addresses, supported chains, API calls, and users. Overage charges apply on usage exceeding the inclusions in your pricing plan.
