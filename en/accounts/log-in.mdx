## Use email and password for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Enter your email address and password, then click **Log In**.

## Use Google Account for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Click **Continue with Google**.

## Use Web3 wallet for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Click **Continue with Web3 Wallet**.

3. Sign the message in your wallet.

## Use Cobo Guard for login

<Note>[Cobo Guard](/en/guard/introduction) is a transaction approval application developed by Cobo that allows you to authorize transactions anytime, anywhere, and securely store your MPC key shares. For more information on how to configure Cobo Guard as a login method, see [Set up Cobo Guard](/en/guard/set-up.mdx).</Note>

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Switch to the **Cobo Guard** tab to log in.

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/account_login_guard.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Ensure that your Cobo Guard is updated to the latest version.

4. Use your Cobo Guard to scan the QR code.

5. Select an account in your Cobo Guard and click **Log In**.
