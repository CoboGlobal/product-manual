## Use email and password for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Enter your email address and password, then click **Log In**.

## Use Google Account for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Click **Continue with Google**.

## Use Web3 wallet for login

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Click **Continue with Web3 Wallet**.

3. Sign the message in your wallet.

## Use Portal Mobile for login

<Note>[Portal Mobile](/en/portal-mobile/introduction) is a transaction approval application developed by Cobo that allows you to authorize transactions anytime, anywhere, and securely store your MPC key shares. For more information on how to configure Portal Mobile as a login method, see [Set up Portal Mobile](/en/guard/set-up).</Note>

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts login page](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. Switch to the **Portal Mobile** tab to log in.

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/account_login_guard.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Ensure that your Portal Mobile is updated to the latest version.

4. Use your Portal Mobile to scan the QR code.

5. Select an account in your Portal Mobile and click **Log In**.
