## Use email and password for sign-up

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and click the **Sign Up** in the top-right corner to **Sign up** page.

2. Enter your email address, then click **Continue**.

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/account_signup_auth0.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Enter the six-digit verification code sent to your registered email, then click **Continue**.

4. Set a password that is at least 8 characters long and includes at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

5. Confirm your password, then click Continue.

After successfully setting your password, you will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. Now you can set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) methods for your account. For more details, refer to [Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) methods](/en/accounts/set-up-mfa).

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/setup_mfa1.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

## Use Google Account for sign-up

1. Go to the [Cobo Accounts main page](https://accounts.cobo.com/) and click the **Sign Up** in the top-right corner to **Sign up** page.

2. Click **Continue with Google**.

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/account_signup_auth0.png" className="screenshot_modal"/>

3. Follow the on-screen instructions and sign up using your Google Account.

After successfully signing up, you will be redirected to the Cobo Accounts dashboard. Now you can set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) methods for your account. For more details, refer to [Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) methods](/en/accounts/set-up-mfa).

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/setup_mfa1.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>
