Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/). You can modify your login methods in the **Login Methods** section.

<img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/login_method.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

- To modify your login password, click **Reset Password**. Currently, the email and password login method does not support modifying the email address.

- To add a new Web3 wallet, click **Add**. Up to 10 addresses can be added to your Cobo Accounts, and deletion is currently not supported.

- Once a Google Account is added, it cannot be replaced or removed.

- For more information on how to reset Cobo Guard, see [Reset Cobo Guard](/en/guard/reset).
