---
title: "Delete Passkeys"
lang: "en"
description: "Learn how to securely delete your Passkeys in Cobo, ensuring your account remains safe while managing your authentication methods effectively."
---

1. Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/), then navigate to the **MFA Management** section..
2. Click **Delete** next to the passkey you want to remove..

    <img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/delete_passkeys.png" className="screenshot_full_screen"/>

3. Complete the MFA verification. Your passkey has now been successfully deleted.
