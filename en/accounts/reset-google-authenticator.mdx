To reset your authenticator app for Cobo Accounts due to device loss or replacement, follow these steps:

1. Log in to [Cobo Accounts](https://accounts.cobo.com/).

2. In the **MFA Management** section, click **Reset** next to **Authenticator App** to display the dialog as the following screenshot shows.

    <img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/reset_authenticator1.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="On-screen instructions for resetting authenticator app" />

3. Open the authenticator app on your new device and scan the QR code, enter the six-digit code from the app on the dialog, and click **Submit** to display the **Security Verification** dialog as follows.

    <img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/reset_authenticator2.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The Choose Security Verification dialog" />

4. On the **Security Verification** dialog, you can choose either of the following two methods:

    **Method 1: Use your old authenticator app device**

    Click **Authenticator App** and the following instructions will be displayed. 

    <img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/reset_authenticator3.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The screenshot of using old authenticator app device to reset" />

    Enter the six-digit code from the old authenticator app.

    <Note>If your old authenticator app is not available, please choose method two which uses Cobo Guard to reset your authenticator app. If you have not set up Cobo Guard, please contact our [support team](mailto:help@cobo.com) to help you reset.</Note>

    **Method 2: Use Cobo Guard**

    Click **Cobo Guard** and the following instructions will be displayed.

    <img src="/cn/images/cobo-accounts/reset_authenticator4.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="The screenshot of using Cobo Guard to reset" />

    Open the Cobo Guard app and approve the modify-MFA method request.

    <img src="/en/images/cobo-accounts/cobo-guard-request.png" className="screenshot_modal" alt="The screenshot of Cobo Guard request" />

    Then return to the Cobo Accounts webpage.

    <Note>If your Cobo Guard is unavailable, please refer to [reset Cobo Guard due to device change or app reinstallation](/en/guard/reset).</Note>

Now your authenticator app has been reset. You can use the new device for MFA.
